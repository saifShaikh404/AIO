Products

  • MATT FINISH

    The onyx is trested with chemicals and abbrasives under a resin line and line plolish machine which creates a matt surface on the onyx with less gloss.

  • CALIBRATED TILES

    Fit tiles in all the finishes and multiple thicknesses(10mm,16mm,20mm,30mm) are processed under a bridge cutter and are under ready to fit the concept.

  • PRECIOUS

    The Himalayan precious slab onyx is one of the most spectacular self-designed onyx. The hard slab is available in different kind of thicknesses, finishes, and sizes.

  • FACADE TILE

    The tiles are available in different patterns, sizes, and thicknesses which can be used as wall-cladding, facade tile, and bathrooms and make to feel nature at home.

  • CUSTOMIZED

    Customized onyx pillars, monuments, statues, and railings with translucent effects are also produced with advanced machinery.

  • MOSIAC

    Small square pieces of the onyx are joint together on a net and are highly suitable for pillars, wall cladding, and bathrooms giving a translucent effect.

  • HIGH GLOSS

    Onyx is polished with the advanced 16 heads automatic line polish machine with imported abrasives gives you the high gloss polish on the onyx.

  • LEPATRO

    Onyx is treated in order to highlight the texture under the 16-head automatic line polish machine. It feels just like touching or walking on leather.

  • ANTIQUE

    The surface of onyx is treated on 16 head automatic line polish machine in order to get a rough-polished surface which gives a sense of antiqueness.

  • HYDRO FINISH

    The material is treated in imported finishing machine in order to get the natural veins and 3D texture of the onyx which creates a translucent and porous effect.

  • SAND FINISH

    The sand is blasted on the onyx with high velocity to create small holes and mild porus surface. It feels like touching sand on touching the onyx.

  • SHOT BLAST

    Steel shots are blasted on the surface with high velocity to create the deep and highly porous effect. The result is natural veins protuding out with diamond shine.
